Support Us

Our publication is managed by volunteers committed to finding and showcasing great literature. Your support helps us pay writers, purchase the tools we need, and expand our reach.

Ways to Support Our Work

Read Our Issues

Our monthly issues are available to read for free with a Kindle Unlimited subscription or for $2.99 each.

Volunteer

Whether it's editorial work, website development, marketing, beta reading, or other publication tasks, we can always use another helping hand. If you have relevant skills or interest, we welcome your help. Feel free to reach out to us through our contact page.

Purchase our Annual Collections

Every year we compile our strongest works into a curated yearly issue. These collections will include exclusive content, artwork, opinion pieces and more, providing a way to support the publication directly while enjoying premium literary content.

Buy Us a Coffee

If you'd like to fuel our late-night editing sessions and help cover operational costs, our Buy Me a Coffee page helps keep us caffeinated and able to continue paying our writers.

Share Our Content

Recommend stories to other readers, engage with our social media posts, direct writers to our submission guidelines, or discuss our published work. Organic promotion through readers remains essential for literary publications and helps us reach more potential contributors and supporters.

Your readership itself constitutes meaningful support. Each person who engages seriously with the work we publish advances our central purpose: bringing you great literature while supporting the writers who create it.
