Submit Your Work

Important

Do not include sensitive personal information in your submissions or cover letters. Please avoid sharing phone numbers, home addresses, social security numbers, financial information, or other highly sensitive data.

For your safety and privacy, only include your name, email address, and relevant writing credentials or publication history in your correspondence with us.

What We're Looking For

Please consult our FAQ for detailed information about the works we are interested in.

Manuscript Requirements

  • Format: Standard manuscript format (double-spaced, 12-point Times New Roman or similar serif font, 1-inch margins)
  • Length: Up to 10,000 words
  • File Type: Microsoft Word (.docx) only
  • File Naming: Please name your file as "LastName_FirstName_StoryTitle.docx"

Cover Letter

Keep your cover letter brief. You may include a synopsis or brief introduction, but this is optional. We prefer to let the work speak for itself.

AI Policy

We accept submissions that have utilized AI tools for research or editing. However, works primarily generated by AI will not be considered. The creative voice must be authentically yours.
