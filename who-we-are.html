Who We Are

Faun by Moonlight is an online literary publication dedicated to showcasing exceptional literary fiction.

We are especially drawn to works that resist simple genre classification and prioritize literary merit and creative exploration. While compelling literary qualities may be found across genres, our focus is on stories where artistic vision and nuanced craftsmanship are paramount.

We celebrate fiction that challenges readers and transcends conventional expectations.

If your work aligns with this vision, we welcome your submission. For more information on our submission process, please see our FAQ section.
