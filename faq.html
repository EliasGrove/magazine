Frequently Asked Questions

Faun by Moonlight is a literary magazine that publishes literary fiction.

We seek works that prioritize artistic merit, complex characterization, and emotional resonance. Our focus is on stories that trust the intelligence of their readers and defy easy categorization.

Literary fiction emphasizes psychological depth and thematic complexity through character-driven narratives.

Our publication's focus is on literary fiction. Your story may contain elements of other genres, but these should only complement and not sustain or justify the work. We're especially interested in works that don't fit easily into commercial genre categories.

Yes. We are committed to discovering and nurturing new voices directly, without traditional publishing gatekeepers.

Yes, we pay contributors for accepted work. We offer $20 for pieces under 5,000 words and $50 for pieces 5,000 words and above. While this is a token amount, it helps writers become credentialed in the paying market, which can be valuable for their writing careers and future submissions to higher-paying publications. We aim to increase these payment rates as our publication grows.

We accept submissions year-round. There are no reading periods.

We aim to respond to all submissions within two weeks.

We publish monthly digital issues featuring 2-3 short stories through Amazon Kindle. Our first issue is scheduled for release in November 2025.

No. There is no fee to submit your work to Faun by Moonlight.

Yes, we accept simultaneous submissions. We only ask that if your story is accepted elsewhere, please notify us immediately by email so we can withdraw it from consideration.

We acquire first serial rights only, meaning we have the right to publish your story for the first time in our magazine. After publication, we retain non-exclusive archival rights to keep your story accessible in our digital archives and back issues. All other rights remain with you, and you're free to republish your work elsewhere after our exclusive period ends.

We request a three-month exclusive period following publication. During this time, your story cannot be republished elsewhere. After three months, you may submit or republish your work anywhere while we maintain our non-exclusive archival rights.
